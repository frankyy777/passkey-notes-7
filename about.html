About Passkey Notes

Passkey Notes is a secure note-taking app where your passkey acts like a digital locker key. It's lightweight, fast, and works on both mobile and desktop.

We believe in privacy-first note keeping with no user tracking or email logins. Just a passkey, and you're good to go.
